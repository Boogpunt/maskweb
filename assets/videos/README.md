# Mask Videos

Place your 3 mask transition videos here:

- `mask1.mp4` — Mask design 1 transition animation
- `mask2.mp4` — Mask design 2 transition animation
- `mask3.mp4` — Mask design 3 transition animation

## Requirements
- Format: MP4 (H.264 recommended for web)
- The videos will loop and play fullscreen as background
- When a person change is detected, the next video triggers
